Hi there! I'm Steven.

I'm a full-stack web developer with an extensive background in mathematics, education, and special education. My passion for teaching and love of technology has led me to San Francisco, where ultimately I would like to marry the two into a role where I can draw from both skill sets.

For the last year, I have been working as a software engineer at RepairPal, an amazing company that connects car owners to our large network of shops across the country. We certify the shops based on strict criteria around customer satisfaction, guaranteed repairs, certification of employees, and pricing. Most of the work I have done has been on the consumer-facing website, creating and modifying experiences for the customer to ask and answer questions about their car, report problems, and get estimates for repairs. I have also done a lot of work around creating and formatting content on the site. For a list of more technical work that I have done, please see my résumé.

Prior to this, I worked at Rocksbox, a jewelry subscription/rental service startup that revolutionizes the way women shop for jewelry. Working on a small engineering team, I have had an opportunity to learn about all different aspects of the business and build and modify features used by customers as well as functional teams throughout the company. Stylists curate boxes using features that I have helped to build. The warehouse is able to receive shipments faster and do regular cycle counts on the inventory due to changes that I have made to the existing processes. Customers are able to more easily make changes to their account using a page reworked by me. These are but a small subset of the changes I have made in my time at Rocksbox.

Outside of work, I love to travel and see new places. In my time in San Francisco, I have found the Bay Area and the West Coast in general fun to explore. Between Big Sur, Lake Tahoe, and Yosemite, it is hard to imagine an area more stacked with beautiful places. I hope to spend some time traveling internationally soon.

Please feel free to peruse the site and try out any of the projects that I have created. I am available if you would like me to answer any questions or if you would just like to chat about the ed-tech industry or programming in general.
