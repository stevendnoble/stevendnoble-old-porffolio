Welcome to my portfolio site!


My name is Steven, and I am a full-stack web developer with an extensive background in mathematics, education, and special education. My passion for teaching and love of technology has led me to San Francisco, where ultimately I would like to marry the two into a role where I can draw from both skill sets.


For the last year, I have been working at Rocksbox, a jewelry subscription/rental service startup that revolutionizes the way women shop for jewelry. Working on a small engineering team, I have had an opportunity to learn about all different aspects of the business and build and modify features used by customers as well as functional teams throughout the company. Stylists curate boxes using features that I have helped to build. The warehouse is able to receive shipments faster and do regular cycle counts on the inventory due to changes that I have made to the existing processes. Customers are able to more easily make changes to their account using a page reworked by me. These are but a small subset of the changes I have made in my time at Rocksbox.


Outside of work, I love to travel and see new places. Being relatively new to San Francisco, the Bay Area has been especially fun to explore. Between Big Sur, Lake Tahoe, and Yosemite, it is hard to imagine an area more stacked with beautiful places. I hope to spend some time in Europe and Asia soon.
